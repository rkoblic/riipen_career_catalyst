# Building Your Team

This is the week your project becomes real. You've been assigned your team, the people you'll work with for the rest of the course on a project for a real employer. By the end of this week, you'll have a working agreement with your team and a professional message to your employer contact through the Riipen platform.

[VIDEO: Your team starts now]

---

## What's due this week

You have two deliverables this week, both completion-based:

1. **Team Charter.** A working agreement your team negotiates together. This covers how you'll communicate, how you'll make decisions, and what you expect from each other. Everyone must agree on it before submission.
2. **Employer Outreach Message.** Your team's first professional correspondence with your employer contact. You'll introduce yourselves, demonstrate awareness of the project, and request the kickoff meeting.

---

## Suggested order of completion

1. Start with the Using AI Responsibly module. It gives you a shared frame for how your team will use AI throughout the course.
2. Then move into your first team meeting and charter negotiation.
3. Draft and send your outreach message to the employer once your team has met and reviewed the project brief together.
4. Begin your organizational research on the employer. You'll want to arrive at the kickoff meeting informed.
5. Review the Communication Skills for Client Meetings page before the kickoff.
6. Look ahead at the Context Analysis & Project Plan rubric so you know where your research is heading.

---

## Before your first meeting

Before you sit down with your team for the first time, take a few minutes to read through your teammates' introduction posts from earlier in the course. You'll be working closely with these people for the rest of the course, and knowing a bit about who they are, what they're interested in, and what they bring to the table will make that first conversation more productive and less awkward.
