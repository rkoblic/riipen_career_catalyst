# Video Script: Your Team Starts Now

In every professional setting, whether it's consulting, product development, nonprofits, or startups, you will work on teams. And the teams that deliver great work aren't the ones with the most talented people. They're the ones that figured out early how to work together.

That's what this week is about. You're going to have conversations that feel small — how should we communicate, who does what, what happens when we disagree. But those conversations are what separate teams that function from teams that fall apart under pressure.

You're also going to write your first professional message to a real employer contact. Not a professor, not a TA. Someone who will judge your team by how you come across. That's a skill you'll use in every job you ever have.

None of this is theoretical. By the end of this week, you'll have a working team and a professional relationship underway. That's real.
