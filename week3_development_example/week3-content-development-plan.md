# Week 3 — Understanding Your Client
## Content Development Plan

---

## Content Architecture

Week 3 is organized around a single anchor event: the employer kickoff meeting. Rather than producing standalone modules for each skill or process step, the instructional content is embedded in the assignment prompts at the point of need. The week's content production reduces to four pieces.

**Pattern:** Overview → Anchor Prompt → What's Next → Reflection

Activity 3.17 (async team communication/admin, 30 min) doesn't require produced content — it's just allocated time.

---

## Content Production Plan

### 1. Week 3 Overview
**Type:** Orientation text  
**Absorbs:** 3.01  
**Format:** AI-generated text (~200 words)

This is kickoff week — here's what's happening, here's the sequence, here's what you'll submit.

### 2. Kickoff Summary Anchor Prompt
**Type:** Assignment prompt with embedded guidance  
**Absorbs:** 3.02, 3.03, 3.04, 3.05, 3.06, 3.07, 3.08, 3.09, 3.10, 3.11, 3.12, 3.13

Organized in before/during/after sections:

**Before the Meeting:**
- Recommended prep steps: consolidate Week 2 research, prioritize questions, assign meeting roles (AI-generated text; includes suggested agenda template)
- Confirming logistics with employer (AI-generated text; includes sample email template)
- Video call etiquette quick reference (curated link + one-sentence framing)
- Using AI for note capture during the meeting (curated link to tool walkthrough + short AI-generated setup guidance)

**During the Meeting:**
- What you're trying to learn — scope, success criteria, contacts, timeline (AI-generated text; framed as a listening checklist)

**After the Meeting:**
- Recommended: debrief immediately while it's fresh (AI-generated text; includes suggested debrief questions)
- Processing your notes into shared understanding (AI-generated text)
- Sending the follow-up email (AI-generated text; includes template)
- Identifying what's confirmed vs. what's still open (AI-generated text)

**The Deliverable:**
- Kickoff Summary format, purpose, completion criteria, AI use policy (AI-generated text)
